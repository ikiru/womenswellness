Patient Responsibilities

Co-pays are due at the time of your visit.

We accept cash, checks, MasterCard, Visa, Discover and American Express.

We require a 24 hours notice for a cancellation. If you are unable to keep your appointment, please contact our office as soon as possible. Our office has a $25 no-show charge.​​

If scheduling a surgery or a new Obstetrical patient visit, we will have you speak to our surgery coordinator on your first visit. Our Surgery coordinator will contact your insurance carrier to obtain eligibility and benefits, Pre-certify if necessary, and then will calculate the amount that you will be responsible for paying. A budget payment plan will be approved for OB patients and other certain situations.

Insurances

With the ever-changing environment of health care today, please be sure to confirm that we are in network with your health care insurance.
